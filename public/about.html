What is Dipy?

Are you in Gothenburg or Stockholm and don't know what to do? We got you covered! Dipy is a mobile-friendly web application that helps you discover spontaneous activities in Gothenburg and Stockholm. By combining location data, randomness, and interactive design, Dipy aims to make it easier — and more fun — to break routines and try something new.

Whether you're looking for a quick solo outing or an activity to enjoy with friends, Dipy provides suggestions tailored to your preferences. With simple filters like area, environment (indoor/outdoor), group size, activity level, and cost, you stay in control while still leaving room for surprise and curiosity.

  • High: Energetic or physically demanding activities like climbing, kayaking, or group sports.

Who created Dipy?

Dipy was created by Esther Lindfors and Tea Klaréus, students in Digital Design at Kristianstad University (HKR), as a graduation project.

The idea came from a desire to explore how digital tools can support spontaneity and curiosity in everyday life. Through design, user testing, and development, Dipy became a platform that encourages exploration and breaks away from routine—while keeping the user in charge.

Future Development

While Dipy currently is in the MVP-stage and only focuses on activity suggestions in Gothenburg and Stockholm, there are several exciting possibilities for the future.

We see potential in expanding Dipy to more cities, integrating real-time data like weather and events, and offering photos and reviews from other users.

We're also interested in exploring how Dipy can foster community engagement — perhaps by allowing users to directly uppload their own favorite activities to the page or discover what others nearby are exploring.

At its core, Dipy will continue to evolve as a playful, flexible tool that encourages exploration, spontaneity, and connection in everyday life.
